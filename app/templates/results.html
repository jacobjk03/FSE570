LLM Analyst Narrative (Llama 3.1)

The AI Analyst Narrative card on the Overview tab is generated by Llama 3.1, an open-weight large language model. The model receives a structured summary of the investigation metrics — entity name, finding counts, risk scores, source breakdown, gap areas — and produces a final structured assessment for analysts. The LLM does not have access to the raw evidence or source documents; it synthesises only the aggregated metrics. All factual claims in the underlying evidence remain independently cited from primary sources (SEC EDGAR, OFAC, CourtListener, GDELT).

Agentic Pipeline

The pipeline runs a multi-round orchestrator — a planner LLM issues tasks, specialist agents execute them, a reflexion layer cross-checks findings, and a stop policy decides when enough evidence has been gathered.
